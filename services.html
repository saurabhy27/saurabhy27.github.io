How I work

Senior backend + AI/LLM engineering, focused on systems that are fast, observable, and maintainable.

What you can expect

  • Architecture-first delivery — clear boundaries, API contracts, and incremental cutovers.
  • Operability by default — metrics/logs/traces, dashboards, and failure-focused debugging.
  • Performance tuning — Redis-style hot paths, schema/query optimization, and cost-aware design.
  • Production-grade AI/LLM integration — evaluation (latency/quality), safe rollouts, and predictable behavior.
  • Reliable data pipelines — Kafka-style event processing and high-velocity system design.

How I collaborate

  • Align on success metrics early (what “good” means in production).
  • Ship in small increments with review checkpoints and rollback plans.
  • Keep communication async-friendly and document key decisions for the team.
